Localtube

Introduction

This software is simply an interface for using youtube-dl (http://yt-dl.org/) as a video feed downloader. You simply enter your youtube channel id and the application will automaticaly download the lattest videos from the channels you follow on youtube. The videos will be saved on your computer as a regular video file.

How to configure

To work the application just need your youtube channel ID. For that you simply need to go into your youtube settings (here, or click on advanced (under your address mail, next to your avatar)), and there you get a few weard strings including the channel ID, the one we are searching for.

If you don't have any channel ID on that page, it may be because you don't have a youtube channel. You will need to create one (even if you don't use it) in order for localtube to be able to fecth your subscribtions.
